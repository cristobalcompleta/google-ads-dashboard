What you're looking at
Today's spend and trials extrapolated to end-of-day, based on how today is pacing vs L4 same-DOW averages.
How the projection works
  1. Actual through the latest completed hour.
  2. Compare to L4 DOW avg at that same hour → pace factor (e.g. 1.15x = 15% above avg).
  3. Apply that factor to the remaining L4 DOW hourly shape.
Metric boxes
  • So far — actual through the latest data hour.
  • Projected EOD — actual + remaining hours at current pace.
  • CAC — Spend ÷ Trials. EOD CAC uses projected totals.
Campaign filter
Filters both spend and trials. CAC reflects only the selected campaigns' efficiency.
Caveat
Early in the day (<8 hours of data), the pace factor is noisy. Trust projections more after midday.
